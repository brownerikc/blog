# About me

I'm a technical writer who, on a whim, decided to start up a blog to serve as an outlet for random bits of writing when I feel compelled to write. It's also a bit of a sandbox for me, as I'm building this using a static site generator that gives me a lot of control over the presentation.

## What I write about

So far, I've focused on professional topics, but that's mostly what's been on my mind lately. I have poorly-formed thoughts about other topics that I want to eventually get to including tech culture, arts, and general life experiences.

## How often I post

Whenever I feel like I have something worthwhile to say, and whenever I can write it in a way that's worthwhile to share. I've deleted more than a few pieces that were about 95% done because I decided they're not worthwhile.

I'm also not really writing for any particular audience. This blog is public, but I'm not intentionally sharing it with anyone (yet). The only one who has any expectations of new posts is me.

So in short, I can't guarantee a frequency.

## My work

Currently, I'm a technical writer at Q2 Software. While I write about professional topics, I'm avoiding specifics about the company, its products, or processes.

Anything I write about here is my view alone and should not be considered the view of my employer.
